Activate Your Credit Card

Activation Steps

Step 1: Open the official credit card website.

Step 2: Login using your username and password.

Step 3: Go to the card activation section.

Step 4: Enter your credit card number.

Step 5: Enter expiry date and CVV.

Step 6: Verify your identity using OTP.

Step 7: Submit the activation request.

Step 8: Wait for confirmation message.

Step 9: Your card is now ready to use.
